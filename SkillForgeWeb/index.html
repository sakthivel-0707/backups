About Skill Forge

 Skill Forge was born out of frustration and hope. In college, the classrooms were teaching us to pass exams, not to build, create, or think like engineers. So we decided to change that, together.

 This community is our answer to a broken system. We share what we learn, teach what we know, and build what we wish we had been taught. Every session, every video, every discussion here is made by students who refused to settle for “just graduates.”

 Our goal is simple: to help every one of our friends walk out as confident engineers, problem solvers, innovators - not just people with a degree.

 Skill Forge isn't a project. It's proof that we can make our own path, and pull others up while we walk it.
